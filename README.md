## About

The Japan Specified Skilled Education Support Association (SGEA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting education, certification, and international cooperation in the field of foreign human resource development.

SGEA is not a supervising organization, registered support organization, sending organization, or Japanese language school.

Our mission is to build a neutral international human resource development platform connecting Japan and partner countries through education, certification, information sharing, and sustainable human resource development.

---

## Mission

Connecting Japan and partner countries through human resource development.
